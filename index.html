Why Morongo Casino Resort & Spa Chose Nestron

A Four Diamond resort. A rigorous evaluation. Nine Legend Two homes. The story behind the largest multi-unit Nestron installation in the United States.

Published by Nestron  ·  Morongo Casino, Resort, and Spa in Cabazon, California

Morongo Casino Resort & Spa doesn't make decisions lightly. As a Four Diamond resort serving more than 10,000 guests daily, the standard for any partnership starts high and the evaluation process reflects that. So when their team began exploring factory-built housing as part of a forward-looking hospitality initiative, they approached it methodically, with high expectations, and no shortcuts.

They traveled around the country consulting with a range of manufacturers to determine who could best meet their requirements. At the end of that process, they chose Nestron.

"When we discovered Nestron, they were by far the best partner for us. First, in terms of quality, second in terms of customization, and third in terms of timeline and being able to do the entire project the way we wanted it done."

Craig Pleva  ·  Chief Operating Officer, Morongo Casino Resort & Spa
9 Legend Two Homes
1 & 2 Bedroom Configurations
#1 Largest US Installation

Nine Nestron Legend Two homes now reside on the Morongo property in Cabazon, California. One is fully set up and open for tours, while eight remain in their original packaging. All are available for purchase. It is the largest multi-unit Nestron installation in the United States to date.

Mike Bean, Morongo’s CEO, reiterated that quality led Morongo's evaluation. "When an organization like Morongo puts their name behind something, we’re protecting a reputation we’ve spent decades building. We knew from the beginning that the product had to hold up to that standard, not only aesthetically, but structurally and operationally."

For Morongo's team, the Legend Two delivered on that expectation. The homes arrive fully factory-integrated, with plumbing, electrical, finished interiors built to a specification that leaves little room for the inconsistency that typically follows site-built construction. Bean felt strongly that for a product category most buyers still encounter for the first time online, seeing the homes in person was the only way to close the gap between expectation and reality. Tom Roger, Morongo's Hotel Director, agreed. "You can look at it online all you want," he said, "but when you have it here on property and you can actually step in, feel it, see it. It's a completely different experience."

"You can look at it online all you want — but when you have it here on property and you can actually step in, feel it, see it. It's a completely different experience."

Tom Roger  ·  Hotel Director, Morongo Casino Resort & Spa

Communication was the other factor Morongo consistently pointed to. Pleva noted that Nestron's responsiveness throughout the process built confidence in a way that product specs alone couldn't. "They always got back to us, answered our questions in a timely manner," Roger added. "You could just tell it was a good partnership."

That relationship carried through to the practical realities of coordinating a multi-unit installation. Shipping, site preparation, and placement at that scale requires the kind of follow-through that either happens or it doesn't. In Morongo's case, it did.

The Southern California desert is not a gentle environment. Sustained heat, intense sun, dry conditions year-round. The units' continued condition in that climate reflects what the manufacturing process actually produces, rather than what a rendering promises.

"The people who come here to see the units are amazed. They make the same comment every time: We saw it online, but this is so much different."

Craig Pleva  ·  Chief Operating Officer, Morongo Casino Resort & Spa

For prospective buyers, the Morongo location offers something genuinely uncommon in this market: a finished, furnished, fully powered unit available to tour by appointment, nearly any day of the week.

Nine Legend Two units are currently available at the Morongo location, in a mix of one- and two-bedroom configurations. For buyers working toward a delivery date, these units are already built, already in the country, and ready for installation once they reach your property.

"If you're seriously evaluating factory-built housing in the U.S., there's no substitute for seeing it. Morongo makes that possible. Go look at it."

Mike Bean  ·  CEO, Nestron
